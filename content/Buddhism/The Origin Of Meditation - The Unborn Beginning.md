**"The origin of Meditation : The Unborn Beginning "**

 Not in heaven did meditation begin,
nor on  earth was it first created.
When mind forgot itself ,
the world appeared.
When the mind turned inward,
meditation appeared .

Thus the Buddha did not begin meditation ,
he revealed the path to recognize it .
Like the sky has no first of being sky,
awareness has no first moment of meditation .
The true begining of meditation 
is the moment we stop searching outside 
and rest in the what has always been.

Before the   Gods and Buddha's ,
beofore the words of Vedas and Dzogchens,
meaning , the nature of mind was already still and awake .

No one created meditation ,
for it is not made by effort or thought .
When beings grew lost in movement  and distraction,
the wise simply pointed back-
not to something new , but to what was never absent .

The orgin of meditation is not in time ,
so no god began it , no Buddha invented it .
Buddha did not started meditation ,
they simply stopped forgetting .
God did not give meditation ,
the nature itself shines without giver or receiver .

Like space , it has no first moment ,
like light , it has no maker .

Think not , who began meditation?
think , who is that seekers a beginning ?
in that thinking and seeing ,
origin and meditater vanish together la.

 Because ,

Without a meditater ,
who can trace an origin ?
Without an orgin ,
what ground can the meditater stand on?
Thus, like two sticks leaning together,
remove one - and both fall.

In the nakeed state of Dharmakaya,
there is no trace to origin and no self to hold meditater.

But trace our own root masters pith instruction,
and hold continue practice of meditation la.
