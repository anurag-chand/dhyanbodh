## Path beyond Meditation 

<figure style="text-align: center;">
  <img src="_resources/lotus-abstract.jpg" style="max-width: 500px; width: 100%; height: auto;">
  <figcaption></figcaption>
</figure>

Beyond Meditator and Meditation.
No meditator, No Meditation .

At first , the meditator grasps meditation as something to do.
In the middle , grasping loosens and meditation becomes effortless presence .
At the end , there is not meditation to do and no meditator to sustain-
rigpa rests in itself , self arising  and self - liberated .

Begining --- recognizing awareness again and again(effort).
Middle --- remaining without distraction (stability).
End --- no remaining , no recognizing - natural state continues by itself (no effort and no doer).

First , meditation feels like resting .
Middle , resting becomes unbroken.
Last , no rest , no disturbance - only natural presence .

First , we observe thoughts.
Middle , thoughts disslove by themself .
Last , no observer , no thoughts to disslove .

At first , we return again and again .
In the middle , there is no leaving .
At the end , there was never coming and going .

Essence of all....
 At first , meditator and meditation seem two.
In the middle , they moves as one flow .
At the end , even one disslove -
no meditator , no meditation , only rigpa of emptiness.
