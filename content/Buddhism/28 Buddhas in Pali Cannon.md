## 28 Buddhas in Pali Cannon

  <img src="_resources/buddha.jpg" style="max-width: 500px; width: 100%; height: auto;">
  <figcaption></figcaption>
</figure>

Here is a concise but structured overview of all 28 Buddhas (अट्ठावीस बुद्धा) from the Khuddaka Nikāya (especially the Buddhavaṃsa). I’ll give key identifiers: era (kappa), birthplace, Bodhi tree, and notable point—enough for study or recitation context.


---

🌼 28 Buddhas – Key Details

1. तण्हंकर बुद्ध (Taṇhaṅkara)

Era: Very ancient (asaṅkheyya kappa)

Notable: Among the earliest Buddhas remembered

Bodhi Tree: Not clearly preserved in common summaries


2. मेधंकर बुद्ध (Medhaṅkara)

Era: Same remote antiquity

Notable: Continued the lineage of awakening in early aeons


3. सरणंकर बुद्ध (Saraṇaṅkara)

Notable: Teacher of refuge (symbolically “saraṇa”)



---

4. दीपंकर बुद्ध (Dīpaṅkara)

Highly important

Gave prediction to ascetic Sumedha (future Gautama Buddha)

Bodhi Tree: Pipphala (often associated)



---

5. कोण्डञ्ञ बुद्ध (Koṇḍañña)

First Buddha of current fortunate aeon (Bhadda Kappa)

Bodhi Tree: Salala


6. मङ्गल बुद्ध (Maṅgala)

Known for teaching auspicious conduct

Bodhi Tree: Nāga tree


7. सुमन बुद्ध (Sumana)

Emphasis: purity and gentleness

Bodhi Tree: Nāga


8. रेवत बुद्ध (Revata)

Known for renunciation

Bodhi Tree: Nāga


9. सोभित बुद्ध (Sobhita)

Meaning: “Radiant”

Bodhi Tree: Nāga



---

10. अनोमदस्सी बुद्ध (Anomadassī)

Meaning: “Supreme vision”

Bodhi Tree: Ajjuna


11. पदुम बुद्ध (Paduma)

Symbol: Lotus (padma)

Bodhi Tree: Mahāsona


12. नारद बुद्ध (Nārada)

Known for teaching widely

Bodhi Tree: Mahāsona


13. पदुमुत्तर बुद्ध (Padumuttara)

Important: Established many aspirational vows

Bodhi Tree: Salala



---

14. सुमेध बुद्ध (Sumedha)

Wisdom-focused Buddha

Bodhi Tree: Nāga


15. सुजात बुद्ध (Sujāta)

Known for noble birth and conduct

Bodhi Tree: Bamboo (Veḷu)


16. पियदस्सी बुद्ध (Piyadassī)

Meaning: “Pleasant to behold”

Bodhi Tree: Kakudha


17. अत्थदस्सी बुद्ध (Atthadassī)

Meaning: “Seer of truth/meaning”

Bodhi Tree: Campaka


18. धम्मदस्सी बुद्ध (Dhammadassī)

Meaning: “Seer of Dhamma”

Bodhi Tree: Bimbijāla



---

19. सिद्धत्थ बुद्ध (Siddhattha)

Meaning: “One who fulfilled the goal”

Bodhi Tree: Kaṇikāra


20. तिस्स बुद्ध (Tissa)

Known for simplicity

Bodhi Tree: Asana


21. फुस्स बुद्ध (Phussa)

Known for radiant energy

Bodhi Tree: Āmalaka



---

22. विपस्सी बुद्ध (Vipassī)

First of the “historically detailed” Buddhas

Bodhi Tree: Pāṭali

Well described in suttas


23. सिखी बुद्ध (Sikhī)

Flame-like wisdom (śikhā = flame)

Bodhi Tree: Puṇḍarīka


24. वेस्सभू बुद्ध (Vessabhū)

Compassionate teacher

Bodhi Tree: Sāla



---

25. ककुसन्ध बुद्ध (Kakusandha)

First Buddha of present Bhadda Kappa (in some traditions counted among last 4)

Bodhi Tree: Sirīsa


26. कोनागमन बुद्ध (Koṇāgamana)

Successor to Kakusandha

Bodhi Tree: Udumbara


27. कस्सप बुद्ध (Kassapa)

Immediate predecessor of Gautama Buddha

Bodhi Tree: Banyan (Nigrodha)



---

28. गोतम बुद्ध (Gotama)

The present historical Buddha: Gautama Buddha

Born: Lumbini

Bodhi Tree: Peepal (Ficus religiosa)

Teaching: Four Noble Truths, Eightfold Path



---

🧠 Observations (important for understanding)

Earlier Buddhas are mythic-cosmic in scale (lifespans of thousands of years)

Later Buddhas (Vipassī onward) have more structured biographies

The tradition emphasizes continuity of enlightenment across vast time cycles .
